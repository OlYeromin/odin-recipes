Fried Cabbage and Egg Noodles

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package egg noodles
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 medium head green cabbage, chopped
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add egg noodles and cook until the pasta is tender yet firm to the bite, about 5 minutes; drain.
  3. Meanwhile, melt butter in a large skillet over low heat.
  4. Add cabbage and season with salt and pepper. Cover and cook until the cabbage begins to brown, 5 to 7 minutes.
  5. Add cooked noodles; cook and stir until the noodles begin to brown, about 5 minutes.
